Facial Reflexology

Modern Facial Reflexology is an amalgamation of methods used by South American Indians, Egyptians and Orientals combined with the latest concepts of neuroanatomy.

R350
per hour

Meridian lines connect acupressure/acupuncture points found throughout the body. There are micro-systems that represent the anatomy of the body. These micro-systems are found in the feet, hands, ear lobes, faces abdomen back, iris scalp, neck to mention a few. The reflex points that represent the body are stimulated by applying finger pressure. The micro-systems function like echo resonators from the skin to the body organs represented on the reflex map on the micro-system. The stimulation has a triggering effect that fires neuronal reflexes. This results in the initiation of pain relief and healing.

What are the benefits of Facial Reflexology?

Effects

  • Relief from: bursitis, arthritis, fibrosis and reflex-muscular spasm caused by trauma.
  • Headaches.
  • Back pain.
  • Diuretic.
  • Inflammation.
  • Hormonal imbalances.
  • Improves immune system.

Good to Know

Another benefit is that if done regularly, stimulation of the face improves circulation, which tightens elasticity of the skin and lessens the wrinkles on the face!
