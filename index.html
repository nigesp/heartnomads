Therapies

Due to tension, toxins and stress, individual organs, glands and parts of the body build up defense mechanisms that hinder normalisation and good health. Reflexology is a series of therapeutic techniques in which simple massage is used to stimulate certain zones of the body, called reflex zones or acupressure points. It is a unique modality that uses pressure from the thumb and fingers to pinpoint and massage reflexes in the feet and face, detecting congestion which unlocks and opens communication thereby encouraging the body to reach balance.
